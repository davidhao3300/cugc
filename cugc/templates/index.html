Welcome to Columbia University Games Club, bringing gaming unplugged to Columbia for over 50 years! Feel free to drop by our weekly meetings on Fridays 8 pm in Lerner SGO Space when you feel like astounding your opponents in your mastery of board games. There are no formalities, no sign-ins - just come in, pick up a game, and play!

Our Board Games Inventory contain everything from family classics like Scrabble and Stratego, to German-style games like Settlers of Catan and Puerto Rico, to thematic games like Lord of the Rings and Betrayal at the House on the Hill.
